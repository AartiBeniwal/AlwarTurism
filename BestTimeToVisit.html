What is the best time to visit Alwar?

Summers in Alwar can become really hot with a record temperature of as high as 50 degrees Celsius. October to March is relatively pleasant. On the occasion of Rakshabandhan, people celebrate by having kite flying competitions and the sky looks like a sea with colourful water creatures. This could be a sight worth witnessing.

More about Best Time to Travel to Alwar

Alwar in Winter (October - February)

Beginning from October, the winter season is the most advisable time for a visit to Alwar. The months from October - February is a nice time for a visit when the climate is pleasant and the ambience lovely. This is also the peak season for travellers.   

Alwar in Monsoon (July - September)

Although the monsoon season receives moderate to heavy rainfall, this is not exactly the most advisable season for a visit, owing to torrential downpours.

Alwar in Summer (March - June)

This is the least advisable time for a visit to this destination, with the temperatures going as high as 43 degrees Celcius. The days are unbearably hot and humid if you're visiting in the months of May & June.
